Bay Wheels May 2020 Trip Data Analysis¶

by Yi Wang¶

Investigation Overview¶

  • To view the bike rides of different hour and day of week
  • To compare the rides of ebikes and docked-bikes
  • To compare the rides of members and customers

Dataset Overview¶

  • May 2020 Data of Bay Wheels Trip service in Bay area, USA
  • Main focus of the data includes: bike types, hour and day of ride, and user types
  • Data covered the registration of rides in 430 stations

Rides of days of week¶

  • More rides during weekends than weekdays
  • Sunday is the busiest time

Members vs. Customers for days of a week¶

  • Both members and customers generally ride more during weekends than weekdays

  • Members ride more than customers during weekdays while customers ride more during weekends

Member vs Customers for different time of a day¶

  • The peak hour of rides is 17 o'clock for both members and customers
  • For both groups of users, the most silent time is midnight ( 3 - 4 o'clock)
  • More rides from members than customers during morning time (4 - 9 o'clock)
  • Customers are more active than members during day time (10 - 18 o'clock)
